Know Your Shape

You are Pear, if

  • Your hips are wider than your shoulder
  • Fat tends to accumulate on your thighs and sometimes the buttocks
  • You have a defined waist
  • Your shoulders are narrow and may be sloped
  • Smaller bust in comparison to the bottom

You are Straight, if

  • Your bust and hips are approximately at the same width
  • You have little or no waist definition
  • Your waist measures from approx 1" to 8" smaller than the bust
  • If you gain weight, typically, the pounds would distribute evenly throughout your body frame

You are Apple, if

  • You carry weight around your mid-section and chest (eg. big bust, protruding tummy)
  • You may have wide or/and fleshy shoulders

You are Hourglass, if

  • Your full hip-line and your full bust-line are the same width
  • Your waist is defined
  • Your waist should be 9" to 12" smaller than your hip or bust measurements.
  • When you gain weight, the fat usually stores evenly throughout your well-proportional curves

You are Inverted Triangle, if

  • Shoulders are noticeably wider than your hip-line
  • Your hips look straight given your shoulders being broad
  • You have little to no waist definition
